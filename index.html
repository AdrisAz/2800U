Who we are

Ontario Food Mart is an online food store created in 2019 whose goal is to help Canadians stay at home and still get food quick. We understand that getting off the couch can be tough, and we're here you help support you in your endeavours. Our simple delivery system and hard working staff won't make you wait for long. What we do

Ontario Food mart is a new company that delivers food right to your doorsteps. We store and take care of the produce in our warehouses daily. All produce needs to be approved and pass a safety and hazard test before being sent out so all deliverables will be safe to consume. Our prices also match those of the cheapest grocery stores, and if you find one where an item goes for less let us know and we'll charge you that stores price. Deliveries are usually between 1-2 hours, and have a delivery rate of 5%. Which is much lower than the average.
